This site is live. Or at least it exists — which is a start.

## What this is

**chromatic-number-8** is my personal corner of the internet. The name comes from graph theory: the *chromatic number* of a graph is the minimum number of colors you need to color its vertices so that no two adjacent vertices share a color. For a complete graph on 8 vertices (K₈), that number is 8.

It felt like a fitting handle — mathematically grounded, a little nerdy, and personal enough that nobody else would take it.

## What you'll find here

This site is a blend of:

- **Music** — original work and what I'm listening to
- **Menus** — food is an event, and I take events seriously
- **Resume** — the professional side of things
- **Ink** — my tattoo journey, with stories
- **Writing** — this. Notes on operations research, culture, music, and whatever I can't stop thinking about

## Who this is for

I'm comfortable sharing this with business contacts *and* strangers, so expect a professional veneer with plenty of personality underneath.
